SocialFix's' ultimate goal is to become pointless by eradicating poverty on Earth

It consist in a social platform where members in need can find around them other members ready to help them with base services. The available services fall in 3 categories: -Offering a meal -Offering a shelter for the night -washing services: Shower, toilet, laundry,...


SocialFix is a non-profit, open-source, and ad-free project.

Anyone is free to consult the sources and reuse them for any project, as long as it is non-profit.

SocialFix aims to follow an anarchist philosophy

Hold on if you think "anarchy" means "chaos". We're talking about the anarchist movement here, which has rules and organisation. The best quote to define it is " order without power". On SocialFix, there is authority stuck in a small group of people, nor any hierarchy whatsoever. Any member must have a direct power of decision and is free to contribute to the project according to her/his skills.
